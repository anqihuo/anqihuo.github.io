Anqi Huo

Hello everyone. My name is Anqi Huo. I am an oboist. Welcome to my website.

I graduated from the Orchestral Faculty at Wuhan Conservatory of Music in 2021. I have studied under the guidance of Prof. Juan Lin and Yi Cao (Konzertexsamen in HMDK Stuttgart).

I am currently looking for a Master of Music opportunity in Germany. Welcome to follow my Youtube channel to learn more about my performances.
